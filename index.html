What If... Game

Gather the team and ask yourselves

... what if ...

  1. the connection between these two components goes down
  2. network connection is unreliable
  3. screen resolution is modified
  4. third-party service is not responding
  5. too many add-ons are installed
  6. multiple tabs are opened
